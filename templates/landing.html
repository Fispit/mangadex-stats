Data Review

The main purpose of the exercise was to plot the weather data of numerous cities around the world when compared to the latitude of the cities. The plotted Data involves the maximum temperature of the location, the cloudiness in the city, the humidity and the wind speed. With these 4 variables plotted against latitude we can clearly see the effects, if any, of latitude on the variables.
